Content Voice Guidelines

Do Say
  • ✓ "AI that takes action" (not "AI that provides insights")
  • ✓ "Autonomous agents" (not "automation tools")
  • ✓ "Production AI since 2018" (credibility)
  • ✓ Specific metrics: "+4%", "85% faster", "30K employees"
  • ✓ Client names as proof: SONY, VOGUE, adidas
Don't Say
  • ✗ "AI-powered" (overused, meaningless)
  • ✗ "Cutting-edge" / "State-of-the-art" (empty)
  • ✗ "Solutions" without specifics
  • ✗ Vague claims: "improve efficiency" (how much?)
  • ✗ Unverified metrics or placeholder names
